Test Your Contact Forms

⏱ 10 min

Why It's Important

A broken contact form means lost leads. You might not even know people are trying to reach you.

Quick Steps:

  1. Fill out each form on your site
  2. Check if you receive the emails
  3. Test from both desktop and mobile
  4. Verify auto-reply emails are working
